The Mold Fiasco Of 2017!

During the summer of 2017, while I was happily working away at my internship, my apartment was the unfortunate victim of black mold... this is the story.

These are pictures taken the day the mold was discovered. It's uncertain just when the shower failed, so we don't know whether it took 2 days or 2 weeks for the mold to get this bad. It wasn't discovered until the apartment management had a meeting in the apartment across the hall and noticed the smell coming from my apartment. Word of advice - Get renter's insurance now if you don't have it already.
