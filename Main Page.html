The Mold Fiasco Of 2017!

During the summer of 2017, while I was happily working away at my internship, my apartment was the unfortunate victim of black mold... this is the story.

Prepare to enter the "Moldy Jungle"...

Timeline

  • July 5-ish : Sometime around this time, the hot water valve in the shower fails... filling the apartment with steam
  • July 5-14 : No air circulation, 80+ degree days, and a steamy environment cause mold to grow uncontrolled
  • July 14 - 10 am : BJB Evanston notices a smell emitting from the apartment and break in
  • July 14 - 2 pm : I get a phone call saying there's been an accident at my apartment
  • July 14 - 10 pm : My boyfriend enters the apartment to take the "before" pictures
  • July 28 : I finally make it back to Chicago to help the Diaster Clean Up Crew go through my things... only 40% is salvageable
  • July 29 : I buy some awesome new furniture to be delivered when my apartment is fixed
  • July 30 - September 9 : I finish my internship, lead KWEST Chile, visit Mexico and Peru, and try to forget about my apartment
  • September 9 : My aparment renovation is delayed and I'm stuck on an air mattress for a few days
  • September 15 : Renovations are complete and me and my new furniture move back in!

I was really lucky to be gone while most of the renovation was taking place. It was a long, stressful period... especially since I wasn't in Chicago and had to depend on friends and family to be my proxy. Love them for it! Now me and my cat are happily moved back in and enjoying the updated space!

Thank You!

I'd really like to give a shout out and a huge thank you to everyone who helped me through the great mold fiasco! First, to BJB Evanston for being incredibly accomidating and gracious. And also for doing such a great job renonovating the place! Second, to USAA Insurance for covering everything and not asking too many questions.
