The Mold Fiasco Of 2017!

During the summer of 2017, while I was happily working away at my internship, my apartment was the unfortunate victim of black mold... this is the story.

All in all, I think I was very fortunate - I had renter's insurance and they happily covered the cost of clean up and replacement. I was able to travel to some amazing places while my apartment was being repaired. I was able to redecorate my apartment after putting it off for years. And they were able to salvage most of the items that were most important to me. These are some of the pictures from my travels and pictures of how the apartment looks now! Still needs a little bit of work, but I'm glad the hard part is over.
